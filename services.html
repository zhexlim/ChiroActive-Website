What we offer

Services at ChiroActive

We use a range of proven techniques tailored to each patient. Every approach is chosen based on what's right for you — not a one-size-fits-all protocol.

Core service

Chiropractic Care

Manual chiropractic adjustments to restore normal joint movement, reduce pain, and improve function. Techniques are chosen based on your specific condition — including gentler, low-force options for those who prefer them.

Learn more
Specialised

Flexion Distraction

A gentle, hands-on spinal decompression technique particularly effective for disc problems, sciatica, and lower back pain. Rhythmic, controlled movements on a specially designed Cox table — no high-velocity manipulation.

Learn more
Core service

Manual Adjustments

Targeted, hands-on spinal and extremity adjustments to restore joint mobility and reduce nerve irritation. A precise, skilled approach refined over 30+ years of practice — tailored to what your body needs on the day.

Learn more
Orthotics

Foot Levelers Orthotics

Custom-made functional orthotics designed to support all three arches of the foot, improving balance and alignment from the ground up. Prescribed and fitted in-clinic after a thorough foot scan and assessment.

Learn more
Specialised technology

K-Laser Therapy

Class IV therapeutic laser treatment that promotes healing, reduces inflammation, and relieves pain using targeted light energy. Non-invasive and drug-free — used for sports injuries, joint pain, and soft tissue conditions.

Learn more
Availability

After-Hours Care

Pain doesn't follow business hours. After-hours appointments are available by arrangement — call or SMS to discuss your situation and we'll do our best to accommodate you outside regular clinic times.
