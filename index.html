Common conditions

Still dealing with pain or stiffness that won't go away?

These are some of the most common problems we help with — and often the ones people have been putting up with far too long.

Lower back pain or sciatica

Ongoing lower back pain, sciatica or stiffness that won't go away can affect how you move, work and live day to day.

Desk-related pain

Long hours at a desk can lead to tightness, discomfort and poor posture that builds up over time.

Headaches & posture

Tension headaches are often connected to neck and posture issues rather than something more serious.

Stiffness & mobility

If your movement feels restricted or tight, it can gradually worsen without the right treatment.

These issues rarely fix themselves — and often get worse over time.

Meet Geoff

Principal Chiropractor & Clinic Founder

Geoff graduated from the Sydney College of Chiropractic in 1990 and has been caring for patients in Adelaide ever since — building a reputation for thoroughness, warmth, and genuine interest in the people he treats.

His approach has never changed: take time to listen, assess properly, and explain clearly. You'll leave knowing exactly what was found, what it means, and what happens next. No jargon, no pressure.

Outside the clinic, Geoff is a father of two grown daughters who enjoys walking, running and climbing Mount Lofty, and making music. He brings the same unhurried, thoughtful approach to life that he brings to his work — and patients notice.
