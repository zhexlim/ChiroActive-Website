Our history

Serving the community since 1994

ChiroActive opened in Kingswood, South Australia in 1994 and has been caring for patients in the Adelaide community ever since. For over 30 years, the clinic has built its reputation one patient at a time — on thoroughness, honesty, and a genuine interest in people's wellbeing.

It has always been a family-run practice. That means a personalised experience, continuity of care, and none of the rushed, conveyor-belt approach that larger clinics can fall into. When you come to ChiroActive, you see the same chiropractor every time.

Your chiropractor

Geoff McCann

Principal Chiropractor & Clinic Founder

Geoff graduated from the Sydney College of Chiropractic in 1990 and has been in clinical practice ever since. He founded ChiroActive in 1994, building the clinic into a trusted part of the Kingswood community over three decades of consistent, patient-centred care.

His approach has never changed: take the time to listen, assess properly, and explain clearly. Before any treatment begins, you'll know exactly what was found, why it matters, and what the recommended plan is. No jargon, no pressure, no assumptions.

Geoff uses a range of techniques tailored to each patient — including manual adjustments, flexion distraction, and K-Laser therapy — choosing the approach that best suits your specific presentation, age, and comfort level.

Outside the clinic, Geoff is a father of two grown daughters who enjoys walking, running, climbing Mount Lofty, and making music. He is registered with AHPRA and is a member of the Chiropractic Association of Australia.
