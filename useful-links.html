Health Funds & Billing

Bupa Health Insurance
Bupa is one of Australia's leading private health insurers. Chiropractic treatment may be covered under Extras cover depending on your policy.
bupa.com.au
HICAPS
HICAPS is the health claims processing system used at ChiroActive. You can claim your health fund rebate on the spot at the time of your appointment.
hicaps.com.au
Participating Health Funds
Check which health funds participate with HICAPS — covering most major Australian private health insurers for on-the-spot claiming.

Professional Bodies & Registration

Chiropractic Association of Australia (CAA)
The CAA is the leading professional association for chiropractors in Australia. Geoff McCann is a member. The CAA provides patient resources and a find-a-chiropractor search.
chiro.org.au
Chiropractic Board of Australia (AHPRA)
The Chiropractic Board of Australia (part of AHPRA) regulates all registered chiropractors in Australia. You can verify a practitioner's registration status here.
chiropracticboard.gov.au
