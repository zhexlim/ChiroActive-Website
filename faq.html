Common questions

Frequently asked questions

What happens at a first appointment?
Your first appointment begins with a thorough consultation — we'll take a full health history, discuss your current problem in detail, and ask about how it's affecting your daily life. This is followed by a physical examination. We'll then explain what we've found and discuss your treatment options before starting any care.
Do I need a referral?
No referral is required. You can book directly with ChiroActive. If your GP has referred you, that's great — but it's not necessary.
Do you use gentle techniques?
Yes. We offer a range of techniques including low-force options that don't involve high-velocity manipulation. If you've had a bad experience elsewhere or are nervous about treatment, please tell us — we'll tailor the approach accordingly.
Can private health insurance be used?
Yes — if your private health cover includes extras (ancillary) cover for chiropractic services, you can claim a rebate. We can process this on the spot via HICAPS. Check your policy for specific details.
How many appointments will I need?
This varies considerably depending on the nature and duration of your problem. We'll give you an honest estimate after your initial assessment. We don't lock anyone into long treatment plans — you're always in control of how you proceed.
Do you treat children and the elderly?
Yes. We treat patients across all age groups. Treatment techniques are always adapted to the individual — gentler approaches are used for older patients or those with specific sensitivities.
Where are you located?
ChiroActive is located in Adelaide's inner southern suburbs. Contact us for the full address and directions. We also offer after-hours appointments by arrangement.
